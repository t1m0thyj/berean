View menu

Go to Reference
Goes to the specified verse.

Go Back
Goes to the previous chapter.

Go Forward
Goes to the next chapter.

Zoom In
Increases the text size.

Zoom Out
Decreases the text size.

Reset Zoom
Resets the text size to the default.

Toolbar
Shows or hides the main toolbar.

Tree Pane
Shows or hides the tree pane.

Search Pane
Shows or hides the search pane.

Notes Pane
Shows or hides the notes pane.
