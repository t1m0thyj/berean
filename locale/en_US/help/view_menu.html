View menu

Go to Reference (Ctrl+F)
Goes to the reference you have specified.

Go Back (Alt+Left)
Goes back to the previous chapter that you viewed. If you click on the dropdown arrow, it will bring up a menu where you can choose any of the last 15 chapters you viewed.

Go Forward (Alt+Right)
Goes forward to next chapter that you viewed. If you click on the dropdown arrow, it will bring up a menu where you can choose any of the next 15 chapters you viewed.

Zoom In (Ctrl++)
Increases the text size in the chapter window.

Zoom Out (Ctrl+-)
Decreases the text size in the chapter window.

Reset Zoom (Ctrl+0)
Resets the text size in the chapter window to the default.

Toolbar
Shows or hides the toolbar.

Tree Pane (Ctrl+Alt+T)
Shows or hides the tree pane.

Search Pane (Ctrl+Alt+S)
Shows or hides the search pane.

Notes Pane (Ctrl+Alt+N)
Shows or hides the notes pane.
